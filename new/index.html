The Specialty Coffee Guide

Launching on the App Store late April, 2016.

Get notified when we are live in the App Store!

Cupper is the 1st specialty coffee app developed by coffee experts to help you find, discover, and experience the highest quality coffee drinks (espresso, latte, pour over, cold brew) and cafe’s near your location.

Cupper provides a discovery platform for consumers to learn about specialty coffee, quality standards, and cafés around them that take pride in dispensing the highest quality coffee drinks to their consumers.

Cupper will launch free on the Apple App Store within the U.S. We have assigned grades to over 650+ coffee drinks and cafes in three major cities in California: San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego. We will be launching in other cities throughout the globe in the coming months.
